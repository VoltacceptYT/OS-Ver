<h1 align="center">How To Use</h1>
## OS-Ver
__Step 1:__
Type in `bash <(curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/VoltacceptYT/OS-Ver/main/check.sh)` in your terminal app.
  
__Step 2:__
Press `RETURN` or `ENTER` and there's your LinuxOS Name and Version.
